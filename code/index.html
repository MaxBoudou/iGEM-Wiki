BACTAIL

Today more and more bacteria develop antimicrobial resistance...

Because of the overuse ,
the antibiotic loses its ability to treatthe bacterial infection.

No new family of antibiotics has been commercialized.
Today antibiotics are considered as one of the biggest
therapeutic advances of the 20thcentury.

10 million deaths could occur annually by 2050, according to the World Health Organization, if effective intervention methods against antimicrobial resistance are not made.

To face this major health issue, we conceived the project BacTail...
